We help truckers find, book & manage loads.

We provide top-notch dispatching services for truckers across the country. Our team of experienced dispatchers work closely with our clients to ensure that their loads are delivered on time and in excellent condition.

We understand that time is of the essence when it comes to transportation, which is why we offer real-time support and keep you updated throughout the entire process.

Fair Forest LLC dispatching service helps truckers by taking the burden of logistics and paperwork off their shoulders. We handle all the administrative tasks including load booking, negotiation, and invoicing, allowing truckers to focus on driving and delivering their loads.

This not only saves time but also helps our truckers maximize their profits by enabling them to take on more loads and enjoy free time.

Fair Forest LLC's dispatching service also provides truckers with access to a wide range of clients and loads, allowing them to choose the ones that best fit their schedule and preferences.

We work with reputable carriers and load brokers, ensuring that our clients have access to high-quality loads with competitive rates.

Our Specialities

  • No Contracts: You're free to work with anyone, we don't lock you in. We retain our customers with excellent service.
  • Efficient Load Management: Our dispatching service helps truckers to manage their loads efficiently. We provide a streamlined system that allows you to quickly and easily find loads that match your route and capacity.
  • Competitive Rates: We offer competitive rates for our dispatching services, helping you to maximize your profits while minimizing your expenses.
  • Dedicated Support Team: Our dedicated support team is always available to answer your questions and address your concerns. We are committed to providing you with the highest level of customer service.
  • Safety and Compliance: We prioritize safety and compliance, ensuring that all loads are transported safely and in compliance with all applicable regulations.
  • Wide Network: Our extensive network of load brokers and carriers ensures that you always have access to a wide range of loads and destinations.
  • Real-Time Updates: We provide real-time updates on load status, allowing you to stay informed throughout the entire transportation process.

How to sign up with us?

To use our truck dispatching services, we need the following documents:

  • A copy of your MC Authority
  • Your insurance certificate and the phone number for your insurance company
  • A signed W-9 form
  • A signed contract for services
  • A complete company profile
  • The name, address, and contact phone number for your factoring company
